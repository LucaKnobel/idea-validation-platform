# How does it work?

<img src="/images/marketing/marketing-process.png" alt="Idea Validation Workflow" width="360" />

Turn assumptions into evidence before building products.

The platform follows Lean Startup principles and helps entrepreneurs, startups, and innovators validate business ideas through a structured and repeatable process.

1. Describe your idea using the Business Model Canvas
2. Define the assumptions that matter most
3. Create hypotheses, metrics, and experiments
4. Collect evidence from real-world validation activities
5. Evaluate your assumptions based on measurable results
6. Refine your idea or adjust your direction based on what you learned

## What you can do

- Structure and document your validation process
- Manage business models, hypotheses, experiments, and results
- Track learning across multiple idea versions
- Build decisions on evidence instead of assumptions
- Create a transparent validation history

## What the platform does not do

- Run experiments automatically
- Generate customer feedback
- Decide whether an idea is good or bad
- Make business decisions for you

The platform is designed to support learning, reduce uncertainty, and help you make better decisions before investing significant time, money, or development effort.
